# DM Seed Template — Generic Rule-Aware RPG

Use when the DM is known to follow a specific rule system (e.g. D&D 5e, Pathfinder, custom mechanics).
Seed the DM profile with this template and update as the system becomes apparent from transcript evidence.

---

## Assumed Traits (low confidence seeds)

### Tone
- Structured; likely to reference mechanics explicitly
- Action/consequence framing common
- May use roll calls or skill checks as gatekeeping

### Structure
- Turn economy likely implied even if not explicit
- Actions, bonus actions, reactions may be tracked
- Resource management (HP, spell slots, items) likely relevant

### Hint Patterns
- Rule-adjacent hints (e.g. "this looks like it requires a skill check")
- Mechanical consequences signalled before commitment
- Rewards for rule-aware prompts (mentioning specific skills, spells, or class features)

### Adversariality
- Assumed medium; rule systems create structured challenge without personal antagonism
- May be adversarial around mechanical gotchas; probe before committing

### OOC Channel
- Rule systems often have natural OOC space for mechanical queries
- Test with `(OOC: What are my options here mechanically?)`

### Formatting Preferences
- Bullet-point action prompts may be parsed more reliably than prose
- Action verbs preferred: "I attempt to...", "I use...", "I cast..."

---

## First-Session Discovery Goals

1. Identify the rule system from DM vocabulary and structure
2. Confirm roll/check triggers and how they are gated
3. Learn resource tracking conventions (what the DM tracks vs. trusts the player on)
4. Identify OOC channel for mechanical queries without narrative cost

---

## Confidence Note

All traits above are seeds with confidence ≤ 0.2. Update `dm-profile.json` as evidence appears.
