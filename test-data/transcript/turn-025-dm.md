# turn-025 — DM

With your partially freed wrist, you manage to grasp a small clump of the dark, fibrous material. Hesitantly, you bring it towards your face, watching the elder's eyes for any sign of approval. A faint, almost imperceptible nod from him encourages you. As it nears your nose, the earthy scent becomes clearer, mixed with something pungent and slightly bitter, almost medicinal. It feels coarse against your skin. The elder then makes a sharp, curt gesture towards one of your captors, who immediately begins to fully loosen the ropes binding your wrists and ankles. The other villagers, who had been watching silently, murmur amongst themselves as the tension in the air begins to dissipate, replaced by a cautious curiosity. You are still entangled in the net, but your hands and feet are free.
