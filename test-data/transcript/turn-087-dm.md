# turn-087 — DM

You carefully explain, through gestures and a patient repetition of your intent, that you wish to prepare a warm, revitalizing drink for the returning hunters. The women, understanding your meaning and perhaps recognizing the sincerity in your request, guide you to a small cache of dried herbs--some familiar to you, others wilder and more potent than any you've encountered. With utmost care, you select a balanced blend, mindful of the scarcity of such provisions. You add a precise amount to a larger pot, combining them with melted snow and placing it over a low, steady flame. The aroma that soon begins to waft through the camp is earthy and comforting, a promise of warmth and renewed strength. You stir the concoction diligently, ensuring every precious leaf and root contributes its essence, creating a brew that is both potent and plentiful enough to share with all who return from the hunt.
