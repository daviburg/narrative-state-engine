To define. --- "We mark what now exists," I state. "So it cannot be lost between seasons." --- I begin, not asking--but naming. --- **Shelter** "One longhouse--central, reinforced, enduring." "Temporary structures--lean-tos, shelters, now secondary." --- **Food** "Cultivated plots--partially successful." "Sheltered growth--proven viable near the longhouse." "Drying racks and kilns--functional." "Fermentation--established." --- **Hunting** "Structured parties." "Pathfinders and watchers." "Mapped herd behavior." --- **Defense** "Perimeter awareness." "Sound traps." "Watch rotations." "Now--pattern disruption fields." --- **Knowledge** "The plague method--proven." "The disruption method--proven." "Teaching--active." "Children learning pattern and discipline." --- **People** "Tribe--stable." "Southern tribes--present, learning." "No internal sickness." "First survivor--alive." --- **Craft** "Basic tools." "Kiln work." "Preservation methods." "Simple ranged weapons." --- **Arcane** "The fragment--contained." "Anya--trained, reliable." "Pattern influence--understood." --- I pause. --- "This is what we have. Name anything I didn't account for. " "If it is not named--it does not exist." "If it is not repeated--it does not last." --- Then I shift. --- "This is what we build next." --- I draw a line in the dirt. A boundary between what is-- and what will be. --- **Year of Growth -- First Cycle** --- "Food must become reliable." "Sheltered cultivation expands." "Seeds are selected and preserved." "Failure is recorded--not repeated." --- "Paths must remain open." "Hunting routes marked." "Travel corridors tested." "Water paths explored." --- "The settlement must expand beyond one structure." "A second structure begins." "Storage separated from living." "Heat and shelter become controlled--not shared by chance." --- "Knowledge must survive the people who hold it." "Methods are repeated without me." "Others teach--without deviation." --- "Defense becomes passive." "Pattern fields placed where needed." "Not everywhere." "Only where they matter." --- "The southern tribes--" I pause slightly. Measured. --- "They do not follow us." "They align with us." --- "They learn." "They return." "They come back stronger." --- "If they survive because of what we teach--" "They will not walk alone." --- Finally-- I rest a hand again against my side. --- "This continues." "Not just in land." "In blood." "In memory." --- I look across the council. --- "We do not wait for the world to decide what we become." "We define it." --- I do not assign tasks. I let each leader answer: - what holds - what fails - what grows - what help they need from me --- "Now," I say quietly, "tell me what stands... and what must begin."
