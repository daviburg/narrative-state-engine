# turn-043 — DM

You continue your methodical work, the small carving tool feeling more natural in your hand with each passing moment. The pile of sorted herbs slowly grows, separated into neat bundles of moonpetal, frost-bite balm, and winter's breath. The quiet rhythm of your hands, synchronized with the women beside you, fills the space. Eventually, the last of the herbs are meticulously sorted and stored away in woven pouches. The women exchange satisfied glances, then turn their attention to a large pot simmering over a smaller fire. A rich, gamey scent wafts through the air, drawing the attention of the entire camp. The elder, who had been observing from his spot by the main bonfire, rises and gestures for everyone to gather. It seems the day's tasks are done, and a communal meal is about to begin.
