# turn-005 — DM

You press deeper into the hushed, snow-laden woods, your elven eyes piercing the gloom with ease, yet the silent world offers no immediate threat. The tracks continue to lead you, a clear line through the pristine white, and you focus on them, your senses stretched for any deviation. Suddenly, a hidden tripwire, cunningly disguised by a dusting of fresh snow, catches your ankle. Before you can react, a heavy, net-like contraption woven from thick, icy vines drops from the boughs above, ensnaring you completely. You hit the ground with a grunt, the impact jarring, and a sharp, bludgeoning blow lands against your shoulder as the net tightens. You're pinned, tangled in the frosty mesh, unable to move freely as the silence of the forest closes back in around you.
