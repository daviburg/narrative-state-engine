# turn-053 — DM

You move a fraction closer, and with a gentle, deliberate motion, you reach out and take the young hunter's hand in a gesture of friendship. He pauses in his task, his gaze flickering from your outstretched hand to your face, a moment of surprise softening the stern lines of his mouth. Slowly, almost deliberately, he reciprocates, his calloused fingers firm and warm against yours. There's a roughness to his palm, indicative of a life spent in labor and the cold, but his grip is steady, not aggressive. As you look at his face, the firelight dances across his features, revealing high cheekbones and a strong jawline, partially obscured by the fur trim of his hood. His eyes, dark and watchful, hold a hint of the same wildness you've seen in the other villagers, but there's also a flicker of curiosity, a nascent acknowledgment of your presence and your quiet efforts. He holds your hand for a moment longer than strictly necessary, then gently releases it, his gaze returning to the smoldering logs.
