# turn-095 — DM

The young hunter is fully engrossed in the task of butchering the deer, his strong, calloused hands moving with practiced efficiency. The rhythmic scrape of his knife against bone and muscle is a primal soundtrack to the camp's preparations. He works alongside his companions, his brow furrowed in concentration, occasionally offering a gruff instruction or nod. You remain close, observing, a silent presence in his focused world. As the heavy hide is finally peeled away, he pauses, flexing his shoulders, a deep sigh escaping his lips. He turns slightly, catching your eye. In that moment, the noisy bustle of the camp seems to fade. Without a word, you step into his space. His arms, still bearing the warmth of his exertion, rise to meet you, encircling your waist in a firm, solid embrace. For a precious few heartbeats, you are held against his sturdy frame, the scent of pine, sweat, and fresh kill mingling around you. He holds you close, a silent acknowledgment of your presence and perhaps, something more, before he gently, almost reluctantly, releases you to return to his duties, though his gaze lingers for a moment longer than before.
