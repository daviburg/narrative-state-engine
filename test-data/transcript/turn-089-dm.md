# turn-089 — DM

The day wears on, marked by the gradual shifting of shadows and the persistent bite of the arctic wind. You tend to the simmering brew, its rich aroma mingling with the scent of woodsmoke. The camp remains a hive of quiet activity, but an underlying tension grows as the light begins to fade. Eventually, as the first stars prick through the deepening twilight, a distant call echoes through the stillness, followed by the crunch of heavy boots on snow. The hunters are returning. A wave of relief washes through the camp as figures emerge from the forest's edge. They are laden with game, their faces chapped and weary but etched with the satisfaction of a successful hunt. Your gaze immediately seeks out the young hunter. He is among the last, dragging a sizable deer behind him, his breath misting in the cold air. As he draws closer, his eyes find yours across the bustling camp. A faint smile, more open than you've seen before, touches his lips, and he offers a slight, almost imperceptible nod of acknowledgment. The scent of success and exhaustion hangs heavy in the air, but also the promise of warmth and sustenance.
