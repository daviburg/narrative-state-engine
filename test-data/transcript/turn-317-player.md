# turn-317 — PLAYER

I do not let the silence linger into comfort. "You have seen enough," I say, my voice steady, carrying across all three. "This place does not exist to be considered endlessly." I look first to Thorne, then Lyra, then Gorok. "Each of you leaves here with a path. Not a question." I raise a single hand, defining the structure. "You choose now what you take with you." I turn to Thorne. "You accept the food and send two to learn. Or you refuse and leave unchanged." Then to Lyra. "You take the records and walk the healing with us. Or you return to your spirits without what we have built." Then to Gorok. "You stay three days with five warriors and test what you doubt. Or you walk away knowing you chose not to see it tested." A brief pause, then the constraint: "No partial answers." "No later decisions." "What you choose now defines how you return--and whether you do." I let my gaze settle across them all. "Speak." I do not move. I do not add. I wait for each of them to answer--one by one--without interruption. What does each say?
