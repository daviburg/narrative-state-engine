# turn-171 — DM

With the blueprints of defense laid and the tribe actively working to secure their future, your focus turns inward, then outward again, towards the most precious part of your legacy: Lyrawyn. You cradle her close, her small form warm against yours, and your senses, attuned to the subtle currents of the arcane, reach out. You feel it--a faint, yet undeniable hum. It is not the raw, untamed force of your own pact-bound magic, nor the earthy, resonant power of the Shaman. Lyrawyn's essence is like a quiet spring, bubbling just beneath the surface, pure and uncorrupted, connected perhaps to the delicate, wild magic of the Feywild that whispers at the edges of your own memory. It is a nascent power, yet to be shaped, a seed of arcane potential lying dormant, waiting for the right conditions to unfurl. She is not merely gifted; she is a bridge, a nascent conduit to the hidden energies of the world, a child of two powerful lineages. The realization fills you with a profound sense of purpose, a quiet joy that echoes the subtle thrumming within your own pregnant belly.
