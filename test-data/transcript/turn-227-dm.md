# turn-227 — DM

The longhouse is quiet, the familiar sounds of the tribe a comforting lullaby as you settle into a long, profound rest. The fragment, its rhythm now a known constant, hums softly on its stone. Winter did not break you; you shaped it, bent its cruel will to your tribe's survival. You reflect on the changes: the purposeful hunts, the enduring crops, the studied mystery of the mountain, Anya's disciplined control, and the tribe's steadfast preparation. The silent star, no longer a distant enigma, feels perfectly aligned. As you drift into sleep, your pact deepens, not with a sudden jolt, but a gentle, inevitable unfurling. The threads of connection, woven through perilous exploration and unwavering dedication, tighten and resonate. Within the depths of your rest, understanding deepens, power clarifies, and something within you sharpens and opens. It is not a gift, but a profound culmination, earned through every challenge survived, every life built, every lesson learned. When you rise, the world feels sharper, the arcane currents more vibrant, and your own power a more potent force. You are not the same as when winter began.
