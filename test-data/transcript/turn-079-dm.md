# turn-079 — DM

Your own experience with hunting is more attuned to tracking arcane energies than tracking game through a frozen wilderness. While you possess keen senses, the specialized skills of these hunters--their knowledge of local game trails, their silent movements through deep snow, their understanding of arctic prey--are clearly beyond your immediate grasp. Attempting to join them now would likely be a hindrance rather than a help. Instead, you turn towards the women who are finishing the provisions. You find the one preparing a bundle for the young hunter. With a quiet grace, you take a waterskin and a pouch of dried meat from her, then approach the young hunter. He is adjusting the binding on his snowshoes, his face set in a look of grim determination. You gently place the bundle in his hands. Before he can fully react, you lean in, pressing a soft, chaste kiss to his cold cheek. His eyes widen slightly in surprise, a faint blush creeping up his neck, and he stands a moment, momentarily startled, before offering a quick, almost shy nod of thanks, his gaze darting away towards the waiting hunting party.
