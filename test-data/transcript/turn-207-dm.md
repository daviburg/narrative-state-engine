# turn-207 — DM

You carve the precise hexagon into the snow, mirroring the melt pattern, and carefully place ice shards at each point. The wrapped fragment pulses faintly at the center of your geometric arrangement. You wait, breath held, for the mountain's next thrum. When it comes, a deep, resonant beat from the peaks, you release a measured pulse of your own pact energy, striving for perfect synchronicity, a reply in its own esoteric language. But the connection doesn't quite land. Your energy, though potent, feels like a misplaced chord in a complex melody, a subtle disharmony that fails to perfectly align with the mountain's rhythm. There's no reciprocal burst of icy clarity, no sudden shift in the hexagonal pattern. The mountain's pulse continues, oblivious to your attempt at a nuanced response. The fragment remains inert within its circle, its faint thrum unwavering, separate from your intention. The subtle resonance you sought, the acknowledgment of symmetry, eludes you. The construct, if it observes, gives no sign of having understood your deliberate reply, maintaining its profound, alien silence, its secrets still tightly guarded.
