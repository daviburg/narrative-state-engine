"Not secret." --- "Guided." --- --- I pause. --- "Before we build further--" "We examine what changed without our intent." --- I do not delegate this. I walk it myself. --- First-- the hardened grass. --- I kneel beside it. Touch it. Press the soil beneath. --- I observe: Does it root deeper? Does it resist cold, or disruption? Does it align with any pattern--or ignore it? --- Then I shift my sight. --- Arcane. --- Does it carry structure? Does it reflect the field--or reject it? --- Then I listen to the small life around it. --- To insects. To grazing animals. --- "Do you choose this?" "Or avoid it?" --- --- Second-- the bird's nest. --- I approach without disturbance. Observe its structure. Its symmetry. Its placement. --- Is it instinct-- or influenced repetition? --- I look with arcane sight. --- Is there pattern embedded in its shape? Or only mimicry of environment? --- Then I call softly to nearby birds. --- "Why here?" "What made this place correct?" --- --- Third-- the rare bloom. --- I study it before touching. --- Where it grows. What surrounds it. What does not. --- Then carefully-- I take only a small part. --- The healer will test its use. --- But first-- I observe. --- Does it pulse? Does it react to proximity? Does it follow the same geometry-- or something new? --- --- Finally-- I step back. --- Not to act. --- To decide. --- "These are not accidents," I say quietly. --- "Either the world is changing--" --- "or we are." --- I return to the council. --- "We do not ignore what we did not intend." --- "We decide if it is growth--" --- "or the beginning of something else."
