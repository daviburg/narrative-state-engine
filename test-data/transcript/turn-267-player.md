# turn-267 — PLAYER

At first light, we leave. No ceremony. No delay. --- We follow the trapper's path east, not rushing, not hiding--moving as those who intend to return. I do not lead from the front. I move where I can see the land, the others, and what changes between them. --- We do not seek the mountain. Not yet. We seek the river bend. The stones. The first place where the pattern touched the world. --- As we travel, I watch for three things: - where life thins or avoids, - where patterns repeat without cause, - and where the land feels... structured instead of grown. --- We stop before nightfall, not at exhaustion. We observe before we rest. We do not assume the world is empty just because it is quiet. --- Show me what we encounter on the first leg of the journey.
