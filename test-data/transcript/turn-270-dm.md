# turn-270 — DM

You establish a camp well above the riverbank, a clear line in the thawing earth marking the boundary. "No one crosses to the water without me," you command, your voice carrying over the roar of the meltwater. From this safe distance, observation begins. Rurik and the tracker move with practiced stealth, circling wide. They confirm the patterns: animals indeed avoid a broad swathe around the river bend, their tracks veering sharply away. The hexagonal melt patterns extend like an invisible fan from the glowing stones, reaching farther than expected, and the crystalline growths form not a simple line, but intricate, branching networks, denser closer to the water, hinting at a web of influence. They mark these zones with cairns and scored earth as they go. At the bank, the stones pulse with a steady, rhythmic blue light. You count cycles, comparing them to the fragment's new resonance. The rhythm here is faster, more intense, but shares an underlying, calculated cadence, like a heartbeat accelerating, yet still bound by the same internal clock. Rurik casts a loose stone. It splashes into the water just short of the pulsing cluster. For a breath, the blue light seems to intensify, flaring briefly before returning to its steady beat. The water around the splash churns, not violently, but with an almost metallic shimmer that dissipates quickly. Nothing else answers. You set two markers upstream and downstream. As debris, chunks of ice, and foamy water pass between them, you observe. Occasionally, a piece of ice, seemingly caught in the luminous field, momentarily gains a sharp, crystalline edge, or a patch of foam seems to coalesce into a brief, geometric shape before dissolving. Through arcane sight, the stones blaze with a structured energy, a complex, flowing pattern that is not identical to the sickness within the man, but undeniably similar in its underlying, alien geometry. This energetic flow does not follow the river's current but cuts across it, a distinct conduit radiating from the stones. A faint, almost imperceptible thread of this energy stretches eastwards, dissolving into the distant haze where the mountain looms. The day ends with a map etched in your mind: a clear zone of influence, emanating from the stones, subtly structuring the world around it, a silent, powerful presence that answers in altered patterns rather than overt hostility.
