# turn-015 — DM

As you are led into the flickering light of the bonfire, the outlines resolve into a rough encampment. Several figures, draped in hides and crude furs, mill about, their faces etched with a wild, wary look. They are clearly human, their features hardened by the harsh arctic environment. Your two captors, as you observe them more closely in the firelight, are robust and lean, their movements suggesting a lifetime spent in the unforgiving wilderness. Their spears, though simple, look well-maintained and sharp. As you are brought closer, you see more details: animal bones strung as decorations, rudimentary tools scattered about, and the distinct, musky scent of unwashed bodies and woodsmoke. One of the figures by the fire, older and with a more commanding presence, turns to face you, his gaze sharp and appraising as your captors push you towards him.
