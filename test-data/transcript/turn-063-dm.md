# turn-063 — DM

You spot a few crude wooden buckets stacked near one of the lean-tos. Picking one up, you head towards the frozen stream where several women are already at work, chipping away at the ice with bone tools to draw water. The air near the stream is even colder, and the effort of breaking through the thick ice makes your hands ache, but you persist. The women acknowledge your presence with quiet nods, their expressions neutral but no longer wary. The rhythmic chipping and splashing of water fills the morning air as you join their silent labor, contributing to the camp's needs.
