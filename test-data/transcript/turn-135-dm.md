# turn-135 — DM

A profound wellspring of joy overwhelms you, and tears, hot and pure, trace paths down your cold cheeks. This is a joy unlike any you've known, a deep, resonant satisfaction not just for yourself, but for this community you've come to call home. You envision the long winters ahead, the gathered supplies safe and dry in the loft, the pungent aroma of smoked meat filling the air, a testament to the tribe's foresight. You see the children, warm and healthy on the raised floor, protected from damp and disease, growing strong beneath a secure roof. As these images fill your mind, a sudden, powerful flutter erupts within your belly, a distinct, insistent kick. It's a jolt of pure, vibrant life, stronger and more defined than anything you've felt before, a clear declaration of presence from the burgeoning life within. You instinctively place a hand over the spot, a soft smile spreading across your face. Whether it will be a boy, a girl, or even twins, remains a wondrous mystery, known only to the spirits and the silent star you once communed with. For now, the strength of that kick is enough, a promise of the future, nestled safe and warm within the heart of your new home.
