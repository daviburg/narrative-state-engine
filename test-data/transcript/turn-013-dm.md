# turn-013 — DM

You trudge onward, the snow muffling your footsteps and those of your captors. The forest grows denser, the moonlight struggling to penetrate the thick canopy above. You notice subtle changes in the terrain: the ground begins to slope gently downwards, and the air grows noticeably colder, carrying a damp, earthy smell beneath the crisp scent of pine. The two figures flanking you remain silent, their movements efficient and practiced. After what feels like an hour, the trees begin to thin slightly, revealing a faint, flickering light ahead. Smoke, thin and blue, curls lazily into the night sky, betraying the presence of a camp or dwelling. Your captors tighten their grip, urging you forward with renewed urgency. As you draw closer, the outline of several crude, snow-covered lean-tos and a roaring bonfire become visible, surrounded by more figures cloaked in furs.
