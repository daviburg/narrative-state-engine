# turn-075 — DM

You finish your meal in comfortable silence beside the young hunter. Once he has emptied his bowl, you gently take it from him along with your own. With a small, appreciative nod to him, you rise and make your way back to the women, who are already gathering the used bowls and utensils. You seamlessly join their efforts, washing the wooden dishes in melted snow, the cold water stinging your fingers but the warmth of the fire still a comforting memory on your back. The task is simple, communal, and a quiet continuation of your integration into the camp's morning rhythm.
