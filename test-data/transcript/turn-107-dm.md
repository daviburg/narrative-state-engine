# turn-107 — DM

The feast is a boisterous affair, a celebration of a successful hunt and communal survival. The rich, smoky taste of roasted venison fills your mouth, a stark contrast to the sparse broth of previous days. Laughter and guttural stories echo around the crackling fire, the hunters reliving their day's triumphs. You eat your fill, occasionally catching the eye of the young hunter across the flames, a silent communication passing between you amidst the din. As the night deepens, the revelry gradually subsides. Families retreat to their furs, conversations dwindle to murmurs, and the once-bright fire settles into glowing embers. The camp grows quiet, the only sounds the rustling of furs and the distant howl of the wind, creating a hushed canvas for your next move.
