# turn-061 — DM

You watch the women move about the camp as the faint light of dawn strengthens. They are already busy, some rekindling smaller cooking fires, others heading towards a nearby frozen stream with buckets for water, their movements efficient and accustomed to the cold. One group is meticulously checking traps just beyond the camp's perimeter, their eyes scanning the snow for disturbances. Another woman is patiently instructing a young boy in the delicate art of carving a fishhook from bone. While the tasks are varied, none immediately strike you as particularly aligned with your own abilities or offering a clear advantage. They seem to require either strenuous physical effort or a learned skill specific to their harsh environment. The general murmur of the waking camp rises, a pragmatic hum of daily life. The elder, now fully awake, watches from his central spot, his gaze sweeping over the bustling activity.
