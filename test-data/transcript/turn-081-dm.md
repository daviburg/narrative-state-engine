# turn-081 — DM

With the hunters disappearing into the snow-draped forest, a quiet industriousness settles over the camp. The women continue with various tasks - mending furs, tending to the smaller children, and processing some of the freshly gathered ice for drinking water. You notice that the elder, who had remained by the main fire, now retreats towards the largest, most ornate of the shelters - the one you observed him entering last night. Shortly after, a figure emerges from the same dwelling, distinct from the others. This individual, neither young nor old, moves with a deliberate, almost ritualistic slowness. Their attire is adorned with intricate bone carvings and feathers, and they carry a staff topped with a polished, obsidian stone that seems to drink the meager daylight. They begin to move among the women, offering quiet words and performing small, symbolic gestures - touching a child's forehead, inspecting a bundle of herbs, or muttering softly over a simmering pot. This figure embodies the spiritual heart of the community, clearly a shaman or medicine person, a conduit between their harsh reality and the unseen forces of the arctic wilds.
