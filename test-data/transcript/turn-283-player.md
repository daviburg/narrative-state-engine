# turn-283 — PLAYER

"We have what we came for," I say quietly, my gaze lingering one last time on the weakened, flickering pulse of the river stones. "We do not press further--not because we cannot, but because we choose where to act." --- I look to Rurik and the others. "We leave no trace." "The disruptions remain--but nothing more." --- We withdraw along the same path. Not hastily. Not slowly. Watching for any change behind us. --- As we move, I commit the geometry, the spacing, and the response of the system fully to memory. Not as observation-- but as method. --- "The river is not our enemy," I say quietly. "It is a machine." "And now we know how it fails." --- We return to the longhouse. Not to report. But to apply. --- "The next step is not here," I conclude. "It is where our people live." "And where this knowledge will matter."
