# turn-009 — DM

The two figures approach cautiously, their movements fluid despite the snow. They wear rough, patched furs, and their faces are grim beneath hoods rimmed with wolf fur. One, broader than the other, points his spear at you again, then grunts, making a circular motion with his hand. You nod slowly, indicating your understanding. They move to the net, pulling it tighter around you, making sure there's no slack for you to maneuver. Your limbs are pressed uncomfortably against your body, but you offer no resistance. They bind your wrists and ankles with coarse rope, supplementing the net's hold, then hoist you roughly to your feet. The broader figure gestures for you to walk between them, deeper into the forest, away from the path. You scan the tree line, assessing the shadows, but no obvious immediate advantage presents itself as they flank you closely.
