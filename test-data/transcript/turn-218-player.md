# turn-218 — PLAYER

Winter holds the land, but within the longhouse, something else has begun to take shape. The fragment has shown its rhythm. Anya has shown control. That is enough. I do not wait for coincidence. I prepare a deliberate test. The Setup Near the longhouse entrance, where the fragment rests, I clear a space. I draw a simple symbol in charcoal upon a flat stone -- not geometric perfection like the mountain, but something intentional, something shaped by thought. I place the fragment at its center. Then I call Anya. The Instruction "You will not reach for the mountain," I tell her quietly. "You will repeat what you can already do." I indicate the symbol. "Trace this in frost. No more. No less." No expansion. No ambition. Only precision. The Control I remain close--not interfering, but ready. The Shaman is present. Kael stands at the edge, watchful. No one else approaches. This is not a spectacle. This is a test. The Condition We perform the action only once. If nothing happens, we stop. If something changes-- we observe, not react. The Intent I do not seek to speak to the mountain. I seek to see whether its presence: alters her control, amplifies it, distorts it, or ignores it entirely. The Wider Awareness At the same time, I instruct the watchers: observe the fragment, observe the frost, observe the air, observe Anya. If the mountain breathes through this place, then this is where we will first feel it. Closing "We do not ask the mountain to answer," I say softly. "We see what changes when we act beneath its gaze."
