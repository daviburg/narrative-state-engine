# turn-109 — DM

In the quiet hush of the post-feast camp, with only the crackle of distant embers and the soft sighs of sleeping villagers, you rise. Your footsteps are feather-light as you navigate the familiar path to where the young hunter sleeps. He is already settled in his furs, a dark silhouette against the deeper shadows, but his eyes open the moment you approach, as if he had been waiting. A hand reaches out to you, a silent invitation. You slip beneath the heavy pelts, feeling the warmth of his body against yours, the scent of woodsmoke and exertion clinging to him. Words are unnecessary, rendered moot by the knowing glances and shared moments of the day. The furs rustle, and the world outside the small, makeshift shelter recedes, leaving only the profound quiet of shared intimacy. In the heart of the tribe, a new bond is forged, not just between two people, but with the very fabric of this harsh, yet welcoming, community.
