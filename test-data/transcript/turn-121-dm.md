# turn-121 — DM

The weeks continue to unfold, weaving you deeper into the fabric of the tribe. Each day, you patiently work with your hunter. You point to yourself, pronouncing "Fenouille," then gesture to him, waiting for a sound that might be his name. Slowly, haltingly, through repetition and shared tasks, you begin to grasp simple words: the sharp sound for 'knife,' the soft one for 'fur,' the guttural growl for 'wolf.' His own name, you learn, is 'Kael.' It feels good on your tongue. As for your other vigil, you observe your body with keen attention. The moon has waxed and waned, completing its cold journey across the arctic sky. You wake one morning, a subtle shift in your senses, a faint tenderness you hadn't noticed before. Your cycle is undeniably late. A quiet, profound certainty blooms within you, a warmth spreading through your core that has nothing to do with the furs or the fire. A new life stirs, a tiny, secret heartbeat growing within you, a true child of the moon and the arctic wild.
