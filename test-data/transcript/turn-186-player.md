# turn-186 — PLAYER

As the silent star's presence deepens within me, I feel my awareness widen beyond the bounds of the longhouse. The voices of the wild stir at the edge of thought, and unseen currents of power shimmer beneath the world's skin. I accept two gifts from the pact. First, I open my mind to the speech of beasts, that no creature of forest or sky remains beyond understanding. Through them, the tribe shall never again be blind to the movements of the world. Second, my sight attunes to hidden forces, allowing me to perceive the lingering echoes of magic wherever they touch the land. I will watch the unseen paths and guard our growing home against powers yet unknown. These gifts I take not for conquest, but for guidance - that our people may grow in harmony with the wider world now awakening around us.
