You see the world not just as solid forms, but as a vibrant interplay of energy, allowing you to perceive the hidden influences and arcane whispers that would otherwise remain veiled. These are powerful gifts, indeed, and you embrace them, not as weapons, but as tools for understanding, for guiding your people in harmony with the wider, awakening world.
