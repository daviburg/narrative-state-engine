# turn-019 — DM

You drop to your knees in the frozen earth, the impact jarring your still-sore shoulder. The elder surveys you for a long moment, his gaze sharp and assessing. He then speaks again, a stream of harsh-sounding words directed at your captors, punctuated by gestures towards you. Your guards respond in kind, explaining something with animated hands. The elder nods slowly, then steps closer, his eyes raking over your warlock attire and elven features. He reaches out a gnarled hand, roughly prodding the bindings on your wrists before gesturing to the fire. A younger woman emerges from the periphery, carrying a steaming wooden bowl, which she places before the elder. He plunges a hand into the bowl, pulling out a handful of some dark, fibrous material, which he then holds out to you.
