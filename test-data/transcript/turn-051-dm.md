# turn-051 — DM

You gather the spare furs, their rough texture surprisingly soft, and move towards the lone hunter tending the fire. As you settle down beside him, you offer a small, friendly smile, then reach out to subtly rearrange some of the smoldering logs, intuitively understanding how to keep the heat radiating outward without consuming the wood too quickly. The young hunter, who had been focused on his task, looks up at your smile, a flicker of surprise in his stern eyes. He offers a brief, almost imperceptible nod in return, his gaze lingering on you for a moment longer than necessary before returning to the fire. The shared warmth of the flames and the quiet companionship create a sense of fragile peace in the otherwise stark wilderness. As the night deepens, only the soft hiss of melting snow and the occasional crackle of burning wood break the silence.
