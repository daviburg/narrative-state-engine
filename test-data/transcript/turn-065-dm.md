# turn-065 — DM

As you chip away at the ice, your senses, sharpened by your elven heritage and the watchful habits of a traveler, sweep over the frozen landscape. Your gaze lingers on a particularly snow-laden juniper bush just a short distance from the stream. It appears to be an ordinary part of the wintry flora, yet a subtle shift in the drift of snow, a faint impression of weight, catches your eye. You discern the almost invisible outline of a figure, clad in white furs that blend seamlessly with the environment. It is a lone hunter, motionless as a statue, his eyes scanning the surrounding woods with an intensity that matches your own. He is a silent guardian, a testament to the villagers' constant vigilance, overseeing the crucial task of gathering water without drawing attention to himself.
