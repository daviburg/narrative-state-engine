# turn-021 — DM

You glance down at your tightly bound wrists, then back up at the elder, raising an eyebrow slightly. He follows your gaze, a flicker of something almost like impatience crossing his rugged face. He grunts again, this time directing a sharp command at one of your captors. The guard kneels, clumsily attempting to untie your bonds enough for you to grasp the offered material, but only manages to loosen one wrist slightly. It's still too constrained to allow free movement. The younger woman, her face impassive, gives no indication of understanding your unspoken question, nor does she offer any explanation. Her eyes, as she looks at you, hold a mix of curiosity and caution, but no hint of shared predicament. She is clearly one of them, merely serving the elder as expected. The elder continues to hold out the dark, fibrous material, his expression unwavering, clearly expecting you to take it.
