# turn-071 — DM

You rise from beside the hunter and move towards the smaller cooking fires, where the women are now actively preparing the morning meal. The lingering aroma of the previous night's stew, now thickened and hearty, mingles with the scent of fresh, unburnt wood. You offer your assistance, and one of the women, the same one who offered you broth last night, gestures for you to help stir the bubbling stew. The rich, earthy aroma fills the crisp morning air as you take up a rough wooden ladle. Once the food is ready, you fill a bowl with the hot stew and add a piece of the fresh, flatbread that is being cooked on hot stones. Carefully, you carry it back to where the young hunter still sits, his spear now gleaming, laid aside. You offer it to him with a gentle smile. He looks up, his eyes meeting yours with a flicker of surprise, then takes the bowl with a curt nod of thanks, the first clear acknowledgment you've received in their language of gestures and unspoken understanding.
