# turn-143 — DM

You bring Lyrawyn to your breast, her tiny mouth instinctively finding purchase. A wave of warmth, deeper and more profound than any magic, washes through you as she suckles, a soft, contented sound filling the quiet space. Kael watches, his expression a mixture of reverence and awe. The midwife tends to you both with gentle hands. Soon, the shaman enters, his movements slow and deliberate. He approaches the birthing alcove, his obsidian staff resting against the hide wall. He doesn't touch Lyrawyn directly, but rather kneels, murmuring ancient words and tracing symbols in the air above her, a soft, earthy glow momentarily illuminating the space. It is a blessing of endurance, of connection to the spirits of the land, a protection against the harshness of the world she has just entered. As he departs, a powerful vision fills your mind. You see Lyrawyn, strong and wise, perhaps combining the arcane gifts of her mother with the primal strength of her father's people. You see more children, their laughter echoing through the longhouse, a thriving lineage born of love and the blending of traditions. The future stretches before you, rich with possibility, and the first gentle tug of Lyrawyn at your breast is a sweet, tangible beginning.
