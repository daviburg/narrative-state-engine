# turn-091 — DM

As the hunters drop their burdens and begin to shed their outer furs, you carefully ladle a steaming cup of the revitalizing brew you prepared. You move through the growing commotion, your eyes fixed on the young hunter as he stands, shoulders slightly slumped with exertion, yet a quiet triumph in his posture. You approach him, and he turns as if sensing your presence. Without a word, you extend the cup, letting your fingers linger against his calloused hand as he takes it. The warmth of the mug passes between your hands, a silent current. You meet his gaze directly, a soft, inviting smile playing on your lips, your eyes holding a clear, unspoken message. For a moment, he is still, his eyes searching yours, and the weariness seems to momentarily fade from his features, replaced by a flicker of something akin to genuine warmth and curiosity.
