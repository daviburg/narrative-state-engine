# turn-179 — DM

The construction of the larger kiln becomes a communal undertaking, but you remain the architect, guiding every placement of stone and scoop of clay. This time, you select a new apprentice, a strong, diligent young man named Borin, who has shown particular aptitude in working with earth and fire. Under your watchful eye, he learns the intricacies of temperature control, the subtle signs of proper firing, and the specific clay mixtures required for larger, more durable vessels. You instruct him patiently, your voice softening with the occasional pangs of late pregnancy, knowing that soon you will need to retreat from such strenuous activity. One evening, as the chill wind whispers secrets around the longhouse, you find a moment of quiet introspection. You close your eyes, reaching inward, allowing your arcane senses to unfurl within your own body. The feeling is distinct, a singular, powerful heartbeat resonating within your womb, accompanied by a vibrant, contained energy. There is no second presence, no dual pulse; only one strong, determined life, growing within you, a promise of a single, cherished child. Later, in the privacy of the loft, Kael's embrace is tender, accustomed now to the curve of your swollen belly. Though your body is focused on the life it carries, your spirit is not diminished. You turn to him, your eyes meeting his in the dim light, and offer a new form of intimacy--a deep, prolonged kiss, a lingering touch, a sharing of whispers and dreams for your coming child, connecting in ways that transcend the physical. His desire, though tempered, remains eager, finding new expression in the quiet devotion of your shared anticipation.
