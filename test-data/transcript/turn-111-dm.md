# turn-111 — DM

Wrapped in the warmth of the furs and his embrace, your thoughts drift to the celestial rhythms you once studied. Through a small gap in the hide covering, you glimpse the moon, a sliver of silver hanging low in the arctic sky, barely a quarter full. Its light is faint, yet your elven senses recall its deeper pull, its ancient connection to life and tides. You ponder your own cycle, the fragile dance of possibility that unfolds with each passing lunar phase. The question hangs in the silent, fur-lined space between you and him, a seed sown in the fertile ground of intimacy. The remainder of the night unfolds in a comforting blur of shared warmth and quiet contentment. His breath is a steady rhythm against your hair, his strong arm a protective weight around you. Time loses its meaning as you drift between sleep and a blissful awareness of his presence, the harsh realities of the arctic world held at bay by the shelter of your shared furs. The night slowly gives way to the first pale hints of dawn, painting the edges of your sanctuary with a soft, pre-morning glow.
