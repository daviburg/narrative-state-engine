# turn-198 — PLAYER

The stillness stretches too long. If the construct has found me, then silence itself is its answer--and silence is not absence. I remain within the circle but shift my awareness outward, searching not for sound but for change. I watch for what the world itself does differently: Does frost creep toward or away from the circle? Does the wind falter or align? Do shadows fall where no light stands? Do my companions' breaths fog slower... or not at all? Slowly, deliberately, I press my bare palm against the obsidian shard and release a second pulse of pact energy--stronger this time, unmistakably intentional. "You see me," I say quietly into the cold air. "Now I will see how you answer." I do not wait passively. I observe the first physical sign of its awareness.
