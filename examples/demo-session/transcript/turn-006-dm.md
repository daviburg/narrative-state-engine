# turn-006 — DM

The innkeeper's expression doesn't change, but her grip on the bar cloth tightens.

"Scholars come and go," she says. "Can't keep track of every one."

She turns to refill a cup at the far end of the bar. One of the villagers at the table — a young man with a bandaged arm — glances up at you briefly, then looks quickly away.

"There's a room if you want it," the innkeeper adds, without turning back to face you. "Payment in advance."
