# turn-004 — DM

The inn is warm and smells of old smoke. Three villagers sit at the far table, their faces turned toward their drinks. Behind the bar stands a heavyset woman with grey-streaked hair and watchful eyes. She studies you for a long moment before speaking.

"We don't get travelers this time of year," she says. "What brings you to Thornhaven?"

She does not offer her name.
